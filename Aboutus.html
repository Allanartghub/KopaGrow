🌱 About Us – Kopa Grow

Empowering Small Businesses to Thrive

At Kopa Grow, we believe that access to finance should never be a barrier to growth. Our platform is built for small business owners, entrepreneurs, and side-hustlers in emerging markets who are brimming with potential — but often overlooked by traditional lenders.

Who We Are

2018 – The Spark

Our founders witnessed firsthand how talented entrepreneurs struggled to access fair financing. The idea for Kopa Grow was born from a desire to bridge this gap.

2019 – Building the Platform

We assembled a passionate team and began building a digital-first lending platform, leveraging alternative data and AI to unlock new opportunities.

2020 – First Loans Disbursed

Kopa Grow launched in pilot markets, empowering our first cohort of entrepreneurs to grow their businesses and impact their communities.

2021–2024 – Scaling Impact

We expanded our reach, refined our technology, and helped thousands of small businesses thrive across emerging markets.

Mary, Nairobi – Grew her retail shop with Kopa Grow's support.

Samuel, Kisumu – Expanded his agribusiness thanks to accessible micro-loans.

Grace, Eldoret – Launched a tailoring business and created jobs in her community.

🌍 Why We Exist

Across many emerging economies, small businesses face the largest funding gap — yet they create the most jobs and drive community-level economic growth. Unfortunately, traditional banking systems often require high collateral or rigid credit histories, which excludes millions of promising entrepreneurs.

🔍 What Makes Us Different

  • 💡 AI-Powered Credit Assessment: We use alternative data like mobile payments, transaction patterns, and social proof to evaluate creditworthiness in real time.
  • 🤝 No Collateral Required: Our micro-loans are unsecured — we believe in potential, not just paperwork.
  • 📱 Seamless Digital Experience: From application to repayment, our platform is mobile-first, intuitive, and fast.
  • 📊 Transparent Terms: Clear fees, no hidden costs, and smart repayment plans tailored to your cash flow.
